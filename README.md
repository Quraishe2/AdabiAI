**AdabiAI** is an AI-powered multilingual classical literature platform designed to preserve, authenticate, and intelligently explore classical poetry across multiple literary traditions.

The project focuses on four major languages:
- Urdu
- Persian (Farsi)
- Punjabi
- Pashto

AdabiAI is developed as part of the **Google Gemini Hackathon**, with a strong emphasis on digital humanities, cultural preservation, and responsible AI.

Classical poetry represents centuries of intellectual, spiritual, and cultural heritage. However, in the digital age, this heritage faces serious challenges:

- Fragmented and unreliable online sources
- Incorrect spellings and broken poetic structures
- Misattribution of couplets and ghazals
- Poor multilingual and cross-era search
- Lack of AI understanding of classical poetic forms

Existing platforms often treat poetry as plain text rather than a structured literary art.
